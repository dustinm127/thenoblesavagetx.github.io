About

The Noble Savage is a cozy neighborhood bar with a dive bar flair—laid-back, welcoming, and proudly unpretentious. We serve cold beer, curated wines, and a crave-worthy menu made from the best ingredients. Bring the family, grab a friend, or come solo—we’ve got a seat for you.

Menu

Burgers, tacos, brisket, and nachos—made to order, fresh every time. Beer and wine only, no frills, all flavor.
